Discover the Smoldering Beauty of Yellowstone National Park

Yellowstone National Park is a 2,219,789-acre wilderness area1that spans almost 3,500 miles and extends into parts of Montana and Idaho. It is one of the largest and most famous national parks in the United States345. The park is home to abundant natural features, including calderas, geysers, hot springs, and wildlife. Yellowstone was the first national park in the country and in the world.
