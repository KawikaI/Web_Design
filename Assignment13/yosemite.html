Discover the glorious heights of Yosemite National Park

Yosemite National Park is a scenic mountain region in east-central California1that covers an area of 1,189 square miles (3,080 square km)21. The park is best known for its waterfalls, granite cliffs, giant sequoias, and glaciers. The park has a wide elevation range and a great variety of flora and fauna. Most of the park is classified as wilderness and is accessible only by foot or horse. The park was designated a UNESCO World Heritage site in 1984.
